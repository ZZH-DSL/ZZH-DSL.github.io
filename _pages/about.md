**欢迎来到我的主页!**

I am a graduate student at **[ShanghaiTech University](https://www.shanghaitech.edu.cn/)** and **[Institute of Mathematical Sciences](https://ims.shanghaitech.edu.cn/)**, majoring in **mathematics**.

I graduated from **[Shanghai Normal University](https://www.shnu.edu.cn/main.htm)** with a bachelor's degree in **Mathematics and Applied Mathematcis (Mathematics, Finance and Artificial Intelligence Experimental Class)**. During those four years, my interest gradually shifted from AI to **pure mathematics**, especially **algebraic geometry**. The title of my undergraduate thesis is **"Geometric Inavriant Theory and Its Application to Moduli Spaces"**. It mainly introduced some basic concepts of Geometric Invariant Theory (GIT) and moduli spaces, along with proofs of several lemmas and calculations of examples. I will later post my defense slides and some content from the thesis in my Blog.

As I haven't started conducting research yet, this homepage **won't contain any academic publications**. However, I will be posting some learning reflections and notes here. Feel free to read and reach out — all math students are welcome.

## My Philosophy 我的生活与研究哲学

在代数几何上我信奉 Grothendieck 的一些理念, 主要是他关于 "The rising sea" 的比喻:

>[!quote] 选自Grothendieck - Récoltes et Semailles, 翻译参考 DeepSeek
>我可以用同样的坚果意象来说明第二种方法:
>第一种类比:我首先想到的是将坚果浸泡在某种软化液体中——为何不直接用水呢? 你偶尔摩擦坚果以促进液体渗透,其余时间则静待其变.经过数周甚至数月, 外壳逐渐变得柔软——当时机成熟,仅需用手轻轻一压,它便会如完美成熟的牛油果般自然裂开!
>几周前, 我的脑海中闪现了另一幅图像.
>那片等待被理解的未知,仿佛一片坚硬的土地或泥灰岩,抗拒着侵入...而海水无声无息地悄然上涨, 看似毫无动静,潮水遥远得几乎听不见声响...但它终将温柔包裹住那顽固的物体.

我认为观念的提升是数学中最为重要的, 也是最为困难的. 想要达到观念的提升, 就得像打游戏一样刷怪升级, 数学人需要去刷掉一个又一个例子, 循着这些例子堆起的几何直观, 才能够进化达到下一个阶段. 抽象和具象是对立统一的.

我的生活哲学是保持健康, 享受充实, 也接受虚无.
